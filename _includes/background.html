Background

Seattle's homeless population ranks 3rd in the US. However, we don't see many foodbanks and it's pretty difficult to find foodbanks that are short on food.
To help with this, I wanted to create an application that would display food banks in a given area. Food banks here would be marked by colors, so food banks short on food would be marked in red, and those with enough food would be marked in green.

I think this idea would be impactful towards the homeless community for multiple reasons.

  1. People can find food banks in Seattle to donate to
  2. People who do not have food can find areas that they can get food from
  3. While this is not the main goal for the app, people can also food banks in Seattle that they can volunteer at

The users of this application could be people willing to donate food, volunteer at local shelters, or people in need of food. I think for now, I would limit the area that people can find food banks in to just Seattle. It will be interactive as a user can enter an area code (for example, "98105"), and foodbanks within the area would show up.
I will be compiling information from FoodPantries.org to create a .csv file of area codes and locations.
The interactions I expect are filtering from locations, and being able to click on a location point to receive more information about the foodbank.
